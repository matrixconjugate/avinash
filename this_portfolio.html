This Portfolio Project

I have been struggling to put together a website because of logistics - I am not a dev/designer. I am a PM. PMs don't really work lol. Jokes apart, things like setting up a database, hosting the project, and making sure you have assets always led to delaying this project.

GPT-4o enters the chat (quite literally)!

I asked GPT-4o that I wanted to put a one pager on GitHub to showcase my projects, and that led to this!

There is a main index.html that references a bunch of content from content.json and other <project>.html files.

It took me 4 hours to put everything together, and I am amazed by GPT-4o's (and in general all other LLMs') performance and cost.
